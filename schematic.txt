  modemBounce  ·  Feather M0 (SAMD21 / ATWINC1500)  ·  Circuit Schematic
==============================================================================

  SSRs: Berm KB25DA (3-32 V DC input, 24-480 V AC / 25 A output, NC type)
  RGB LED: common cathode; single 470 ohm resistor on cathode path
  MCU power: disassembled USB wall-wart wired internally to post-fuse AC;
             5 V DC output wired directly to Feather VUSB header pin
  Construction: hand-assembled protoboard PCB

  NORMALLY-CLOSED SSR LOGIC
  -------------------------
  The Berm KB25DA has both normally-closed (NC) and normally-open (NO)
  pins, but NC mode is used exclusively: AC passes through by default.
  The Feather only has to act to interrupt power, not to restore it.
  If the Feather loses power or crashes, both outlets remain energized.

    Feather pin LOW  (0 V)   -> Q off -> SSR de-energized -> CLOSED -> AC ON
    Feather pin HIGH (3.3 V) -> Q sat -> SSR energized    -> OPEN   -> AC OFF

  Both SSR output pins are initialized LOW in setup(), so AC is on at boot.


  ENCLOSURE
  ---------
  Entirely laser-cut Delrin (acetal/POM) — electrically insulating and
  self-extinguishing. Three internal compartments provide HV/LV isolation:

    [Low-voltage]          [High-voltage mixed]        [AC outlets]
    Protoboard PCB         SSRs                        Modem plug
    Feather M0             USB wall-wart               Router plug
    LED, button            Incoming mains cable        10 A fuse

  The two internal divider panels (interior-short, interior-long) physically
  separate the low-voltage PCB from all mains-potential conductors.


  AC SAFETY NOTE
  --------------
  The 10 A fuse protects the entire hot feed before it reaches the SSRs or
  the internal wall-wart. Neutral is passed through uninterrupted. All
  mains-potential conductors are contained within the two HV compartments
  and are physically isolated from the low-voltage PCB by Delrin dividers.
